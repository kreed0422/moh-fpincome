Information Collection Notice

The personal information you provide to the PharmaCare program will be used by the British Columbia Ministry of Health to verify and administer your Fair PharmaCare coverage. It will be collected, used and disclosed in accordance with the Pharmaceutical Services Act and the Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act. For any questions about the collection or use of this information, call Health Insurance BC from Vancouver at 1-604-683-7151 or from elsewhere in B.C. toll free at 1-800-663-7100.

Get Started

If you are registered for Fair PharmaCare, you can apply to have your coverage reviewed if your income has dropped by 10% or more in the past two years. It is possible that your deductible and family maximum will be lowered.

You can use this form if

  • You are registered for Fair PharmaCare and your registration status is complete. If you recently registered, wait to receive a Confirmation of Assistance letter. If you aren't sure, check your status.
  • You expect your net income for last year or gross income for this year to be at least 10% lower than your net income from two years ago. The loss of income can be for any reason, including the COVID-19 pandemic.
Note: Submit this application no later than December 31 of this year. We only review your coverage for this year. Your coverage for previous years cannot be reviewed or changed retroactively.
New residents of Canada: If you have not yet filed a tax return in Canada for a full year of income, you must submit a Notarized Affidavit Form (HLTH 5357) before submitting this application. Call Health Insurance BC (HIBC) to request an affidavit form: 1-800-663-7100 (toll-free); 604-683-7151 (Lower Mainland).
Did you know: The Medical Services Plan (MSP) can help households with lower incomes pay for certain supplementary medical services (e.g., physical therapy, massage, podiatry). Learn more about MSP supplementary benefits.

Fair PharmaCare Registration

Income reviews are for people who are registered for the income-based Fair PharmaCare plan. You can register here. You can only apply for an income review once you have registered for Fair PharmaCare and received a Confirmation of Assistance letter.

Income Requirements

Income reviews are only for Fair PharmaCare registrants whose income has dropped by 10% or more since it was verified two years ago.
Net income: Your income after deductions. We use your Canada Revenue Agency Notice of Assessment / Reassessment to verify your net income. Contact HIBC if you don't have a Notice of Assessment / Reassessment.
Gross income: Your income before deductions, verified by supporting documents.
